Let us learn some new facts today

  • Octopuses have three hearts: Two of these hearts pump blood through the gills, while the third circulates blood to the rest of the body.
  • A group of owls is called a parliament: This term is used to describe a gathering of these wise-looking birds.
  • Hot water may freeze faster than cold water: This phenomenon, known as the Mpemba effect, is still being researched.
  • The shortest war in history lasted only 38 minutes: The Anglo-Zanzibar War in 1896 holds this record.
  • Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't: Botanically speaking, a berry is defined as a fruit with seeds embedded in its flesh, which fits bananas but not strawberries.
